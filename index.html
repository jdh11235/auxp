Serialist Chromatic Octave

Seralism is a method of composing based on patterns. In this example, all the notes in a range must be played exactly once before they can be played again.
 
There are 11 factorial or 39,916,800 possible arrangements of the 12 notes in a one-octave chromatic scale (if always starting on the same note).
